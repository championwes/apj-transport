About Us

Owner-Operated. Personally Accountable.

Andrew P Jacobs Transport Inc is an owner-operated freight carrier based in Lumberton, North Carolina. We specialize in moving general freight, building materials, lumber, and heavy equipment across the Southeast and throughout the continental United States.

As a small, hands-on operation, every load gets personal attention from dispatch to delivery. Our spotless safety record isn't a marketing claim — it's a reflection of how we run every single mile.
